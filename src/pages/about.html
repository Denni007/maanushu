The Trusted Name in Bearings and Industrial Lubricants

Established in 1997, Maanush Corporation is a leading supplier of all kinds of bearings and industrial lubricants. Located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we have been serving both the Indian and Export markets, continuously expanding our reach and offerings. With years of dedicated service, we have become a trusted name, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

What Is the Our Products?

We take pride in offering a comprehensive range of industrial lubricants that cater to various segments of the industry. Our product lineup includes hydraulic oils, industrial gear oils, general machinery oils, heat transfer oils, extreme pressure greases, quenching oils, pneumatic oils, neat and soluble cutting oils, gas engine oils, marine oils, rust preventives, and specialty products. Additionally, we offer a wide range of automotive lubricants, ranging from two-wheeler oils to gear oils and greases.

Specialization in Bearings

At Maanush Corporation, we are specialists in all kinds of heavy, odd, and large bearings. Our expertise extends to providing non-standard sizes, costly bearings, brass cage bearings, and rare bearings of any type as per our customer's specific requirements. With an unwavering commitment to meeting our customers' needs, we are proficient in supplying large bearings and discontinued industrial bearings that are hard to find anywhere else.
